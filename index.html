online branding

Practice of establishing and promoting a brand or company's image, reputation, and values through various online channels such as websites, social media, email marketing, search engines, and online advertising.

An effective online branding strategy can help increase brand awareness, build brand loyalty, and drive customer engagement and conversion rates. It also allows brands to stay relevant in a digital age where consumers spend more time online than ever before

animated ads

Are online advertising campaigns that use moving graphics or animations to capture the viewer's attention and communicate a message. These ads can be in various forms such as GIFs, videos, or other animated formats

Animated ads are popular because they can be more engaging and visually appealing than static ads, making them more effective in driving clicks, views, and conversions. They can also convey complex ideas or product features in a more visually appealing way than text-based ads.
